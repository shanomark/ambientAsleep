Ambient Asleep Privacy Policy

I. Introduction

Welcome to use "Ambient Asleep". We attach great importance to user privacy and are committed to protecting the security of your personal information during the use of this application. This privacy policy will explain to you how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information.

II. Information Collection

1. We may collect your device number. The collection of this information is only for analyzing application crashes and continuously improving the functions of "Ambient Asleep" to ensure a more stable and high-quality sleep assistance experience for you.

2. We collect personal information only within the necessary scope and will ensure the accuracy and completeness of the collected information.

III. Information Use

1. The device number will be used to diagnose and solve possible application crashes so that we can take prompt measures to repair and improve the stability of the application.

2. We will also use this information to optimize various functions of the application and make targeted improvements according to the characteristics of different devices to better meet your sleep needs.

IV. Information Storage

1. We will store your device number in a secure way, adopt advanced encryption technology and strict access control measures to ensure that your personal information is not accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed without authorization.

2. We will determine the storage period of personal information according to applicable laws, regulations and internal policies. After the expiration of the storage period, we will take appropriate measures to delete or anonymize your personal information.

V. Information Security

1. We will fully protect the security of your personal information. Adopt multiple security protection measures, including but not limited to data encryption, network security monitoring, and regular security audits to prevent possible security risks.

2. Our development team will continuously pay attention to the latest security technology trends and continuously upgrade and improve our security measures to ensure that your information is always under secure protection.

VI. Information Sharing Commitment

Please rest assured that we solemnly promise that any information collected by you, such as your device number, will never be shared with any third party. We always take protecting your privacy as our top priority, strictly abide by relevant laws and regulations, and ensure the security of your personal information.

VII. User Rights

1. You have the right to know at any time what personal information we have collected about you and the specific way these information are used.

2. If you find that there are inaccuracies in your personal information, you can ask us to correct it. Under certain circumstances, you can also ask us to delete your personal information.
